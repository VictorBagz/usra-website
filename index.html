About USRA

Our Story

Founded in the late 1990s by a small group of passionate teachers from pioneering schools including St. Mary's College Kisubi (SMACK), Namilyango College, and King's College Budo, the Uganda Schools Rugby Association (USRA) began as a grassroots movement to introduce rugby into schools. What started with a handful of enthusiastic teams has grown into a nationwide organization overseeing the development and management of both boys’ and girls’ rugby across both the 15s and 7s formats—in primary and secondary schools throughout Uganda.

Over the years, USRA has been instrumental in nurturing young talent, establishing structured competitions, and building a strong foundation for the sport at the school level, contributing significantly to the national rugby ecosystem.

Our Mission

To promote and develop rugby in schools nationwide, providing opportunities for student athletes to grow, learn, and compete in a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment.

Our Vision

To foster a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and teamwork in school rugby, developing well-rounded individuals who excel on and off the pitch.

Our Goals

The objectives that drive our mission forward.

  1. Foster a love for the game and promote rugby development programs
  2. Provide training, resources, and support for coaches, players, and officials
  3. Organize competitive tournaments and events that showcase student-athlete talent
  4. Emphasize sportsmanship, discipline, and respect among young student athletes.
  5. Collaborate with schools, local communities, and rugby organizations to grow the sport
  6. Empower the next generation of rugby players, leaders, and citizens through local and international exposure

Chairman's Message

Mr. Okello Dickson Odyek

Dear Rugby Enthusiasts, all Stakeholders and Partners; It is with great pride and excitement that I welcome you to the official website of the Uganda Schools Rugby Association (USRA). This platform marks a significant milestone in our journey to grow, promote, and professionalize school rugby across Uganda.

The development of this website reflects our commitment to transparency, communication, and engagement with all our stakeholders—from students and coaches to parents, alumni, rugby fans and sponsors. Here, you will find up-to-date information on fixtures, results, tournaments, development programs, and key initiatives aimed at nurturing young Rugby talent in Uganda at school level. We strongly believe that rugby, a fast growing sports in schools, is not just a game but a powerful tool for character building, discipline, teamwork, and national pride. Through this digital platform, we aim to share the inspiring stories from our schools, highlight achievements, and provide resources that support the holistic development of our players.

On behalf of the USRA Executive Committee, and on behalf of all our membership, I thank all who have contributed to making this vision a reality. We invite you to explore, engage, and grow with us as we take schools rugby to greater heights.

Okello Dickson

Chairman, Uganda Schools Rugby Association

Welfare & Safety

Athletes Medical Fund

The Athletes Medical Fund supports student rugby players who suffer injuries while representing their schools. It provides timely medical assistance, injury management, and rehabilitation support so that our athletes can recover safely and return to the sport they love.

  • Emergency medical support during competitions
  • Subsidized care for approved injuries
  • Clear claim and verification process via USRA
