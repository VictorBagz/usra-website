Our Journey

The USRA Story

Founded in the late 1990s by passionate educators from pioneering schools including St. Mary's College Kisubi (SMACK), Namilyango College, and King's College Budo, the Uganda Schools Rugby Association began as a grassroots movement to introduce rugby into schools.

What started with a handful of enthusiastic teams has grown into a nationwide organization overseeing the development and management of both boys' and girls' rugby across 15s and 7s formats in primary and secondary schools throughout Uganda.

Over the years, USRA has been instrumental in nurturing young talent, establishing structured competitions, and building a strong foundation for the sport at school level, contributing significantly to Uganda's national rugby ecosystem.

Our Mission

To promote and develop rugby in schools nationwide, providing opportunities for student athletes to grow, learn, and compete in a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment.

Our Vision

To foster a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and teamwork in school rugby, developing well-rounded individuals who excel on and off the pitch.

Welfare & Safety

Athletes Medical Fund

The Athletes Medical Fund supports student rugby players who suffer injuries while representing their schools. It provides timely medical assistance, injury management, and rehabilitation support so that our athletes can recover safely and return to the sport they love.

  • Emergency medical support during competitions
  • Subsidized care for approved injuries
  • Clear claim and verification process via USRA
